About Myss T

I Walked Away to Rebuild — On My Own Terms

Shatica Miles — known as Myss T — is a creator and entrepreneur who left a 23-year career to rebuild life on her own terms. She is the voice behind Whose Tea TV, where lived experience meets real change.

Through Whose Tea TV and her Garden to Skin brand, she turns real life shifts into clear, usable growth — empowering people to stop performing and start living with intention.
